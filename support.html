Frequently asked

Is the app free?

Yes — The Rogue's Gallery is free to download and play on the App Store. Some optional extras and ads help support development.

Do I need an account?

No. You can play right away with no sign-up. Your progress is saved on your device.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Every game plays fully offline. Stats and coins earned offline sync to your account automatically once you reconnect.

Is it on Android?

Not yet — The Rogue's Gallery is currently available on iPhone and iPad. Follow along for news on future platforms.
